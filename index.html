How It Works

Walk. Arrive. Listen.

01

Open the app

Open TourTour before you head out. No routes to plan, nothing to search. Just start walking wherever you want to go.

02

TourTour notices first

As you approach a landmark, TourTour detects it. By the time you arrive your guide is already ready — no loading, no waiting.

03

Hear the story

A personalised audio guide plays automatically. Eyes up, phone in pocket. The story comes to you.

04

Keep exploring

Walk on. Every new landmark triggers a new guide. No app to re-open, no map to consult. The city unfolds as you move through it.

Features

Every other guide makes you do the work

Completely Automatic

No QR codes, no menus, no searching. TourTour knows where you are and starts your guide the moment you arrive. Walk past something interesting and it tells you about it.

Guides That Sound Human

Not a Wikipedia article read back at you. TourTour generates a narrative specifically for each landmark — the context, the stories, the things worth knowing when you're standing right there.

Instant, Even Without Signal

TourTour prepares guides for the places you're heading before you get there. In a narrow alley in an old city with no signal, your guide plays without a pause.
